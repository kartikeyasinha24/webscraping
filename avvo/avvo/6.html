About Lisa

I am an attorney born and raised in Jasper, Alabama.  My practice focuses on appellate law, criminal law, personal injury, and legal research and writing.

 

My staff consists of my office cat, Ellie, who is very shy but sweet with my clients.  I am an advocate for diabetes prevention and cure, and enjoy photography, classic cars, and tennis in my spare time.
